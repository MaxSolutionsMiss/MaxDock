MaxDock v46-DB25 Deployment

Changes
- Retains the established full-interface color theme for each selected MaxDock location.
- Condenses the appointment modal to reduce vertical scrolling on desktop and mobile screens.
- Moves the compact template selector and Use/Delete controls into the modal header.
- Replaces the visible origin field with the automatically assigned current location.
- Makes every new booking outbound from that assigned origin.
- Adds a Destination Type choice for Max Solutions, Customer, or Vendor.
- Lists active MaxDock sites when Max Solutions is selected and opens a destination-name field for Customer or Vendor.
- Removes Other Sister Plant and other ambiguous free-text destination categories.
- Shows the signed-in requester's name and email automatically and pre-fills the Contact step.
- Adds WIP and VIP to the appointment-type list at every active location.
- Stops the five-second live refresh from rebuilding Pick an Available Time while the user is choosing a slot.
- Shows return-load intelligence in a prominent Opportunity for Efficiency pop-up.
- Keeps the destination's reciprocal schedule view automatic and read-only.

Database
- Apply MaxDock_DB_v17_WIP_Appointment_Type.sql using migration name maxdock_db25_wip_appointment_type.
- Apply MaxDock_DB_v17a_VIP_Appointment_Type.sql using migration name maxdock_db25_vip_appointment_type.
- The migrations add WIP and VIP to the appointment-type master and enable both for every active location.

Edge Functions
- No Edge Function deployment is required for DB25.

Frontend
- Publish all changed root files and their mirrored db04 copies.
- Hard-refresh the browser after GitHub Pages finishes deploying.

Verification
1. Switch among several locations and confirm each established location theme remains active across the interface.
2. Open Place Appointment and confirm the compact modal does not show a separate origin field.
3. Confirm Destination Type offers Max Solutions, Customer, and Vendor, with no Other Sister Plant choice.
4. Confirm Max Solutions lists active sites except the origin, while Customer and Vendor open a destination-name field.
5. Confirm the route is always outbound from the assigned origin and a Max Solutions destination sees the linked movement as inbound.
6. Confirm Requester shows the signed-in user's name and email and the same fields are pre-filled in Contact and Reference.
7. Confirm WIP and VIP appear in Appointment Type and produce valid available slots and bookings.
8. Leave Pick an Available Time open for at least 15 seconds and confirm the slot list does not rebuild or jump.
9. Select a time with a reverse-route match and confirm Opportunity for Efficiency opens as a pop-up and remains advisory only.
