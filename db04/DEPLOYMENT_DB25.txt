MaxDock v46-DB25 Deployment

Changes
- Keeps the Mississauga navy, blue, and teal theme throughout MaxDock at every selected location.
- Limits location-specific color to the Location selector in the top header.
- Simplifies booking to Sending From, Sending To, and a fixed outbound movement.
- Uses the current top-header location as the automatically selected origin.
- Limits Sending To to active MaxDock destinations and removes requester-type and free-text sister-plant choices.
- Shows the signed-in requester's name and email automatically and pre-fills the Contact step.
- Adds WIP to the appointment-type list at every active location.
- Stops the five-second live refresh from rebuilding Pick an Available Time while the user is choosing a slot.
- Shows return-load intelligence in a prominent Opportunity for Efficiency pop-up.
- Keeps the destination's reciprocal schedule view automatic and read-only.

Database
- Apply MaxDock_DB_v17_WIP_Appointment_Type.sql using migration name maxdock_db25_wip_appointment_type.
- The migration adds WIP to the appointment-type master and enables it for every active location.

Edge Functions

Frontend

Verification

2. Open Place Appointment and confirm Sending From matches the selected top location and cannot be changed inside the booking.

4. Confirm the route is always outbound from the origin and appears as inbound on the destination's linked schedule.
5. Confirm Requester shows the signed-in user's name and email and the same fields are pre-filled in Contact and Reference.
6. Confirm WIP appears in Appointment Type and produces valid available slots and bookings.
