MaxDock DB58 — Balanced KPI spacing and inline operational filters

- Uses 64px KPI cards on compact screens.
- Vertically centres the icon and the combined number/description block.

- Shows five My Appointments KPI cards across the desktop workspace.
- Places operational filter labels directly in front of their fields on Dashboard, Queue, Reports, and My Appointments.
- Keeps horizontal label-to-field pairs while allowing responsive wrapping on small screens.
