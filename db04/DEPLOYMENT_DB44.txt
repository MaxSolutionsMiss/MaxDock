MaxDock v65-DB44 — Coloured Identity and Stable Navigation

Purpose
- Restore the approved coloured Max mark while retaining DB43's flat operational-console discipline.
- Keep the authenticated header in the same position and proportions on every page.
- Improve booking-step scan speed and reduce permanent density in User Management.

Brand and Page Canvas
- The Max mark again uses the earlier blue-to-green Max gradient with the white symbol.
- The old logo shadow is not restored.
- MaxDock is slightly larger and Max Solutions Packaging Inc. appears directly below it in authenticated headers.
- Sign-in and password setup use the same coloured mark, larger MaxDock wordmark, and company attribution.
- Operational pages use a subtle Max blue/green background wash with flat bordered content surfaces.

Navigation and Operations Controls
- Desktop headers use stable brand, navigation, and account-control lanes so menu positions do not jump between pages.
- Persistent navigation remains role-aware and the gear menu remains available for secondary and narrow-screen navigation.
- Dashboard and Operations Queue toolbars retain their shared compact layout and explicitly protect gear menus from clipping.
- Tablet and mobile breakpoints collapse navigation before controls can overlap.

Customer Booking
- Step numbers 1 through 5 are larger.
- Step labels are shifted left and aligned beside their number for faster scanning.
- Mobile steps retain a compact centered stack for readability.
- Booking logic, appointment fields, permissions, and slot selection are unchanged.

User Management
- A left-side user-group navigator separates All Users, Internal Team, Customers, Vendors, and Setup Required.
- The primary table no longer displays a permanent Locations column.
- Details expands the selected user in place and reveals location access, customer/vendor identity, and username.
- Search still includes hidden location and company values.
- Add, edit, setup-link, reset-password, deletion, privilege, and location-assignment behavior is unchanged.

Database and Edge Functions
- No Supabase migration is required.
- No Edge Function deployment is required.
- Scheduling, live refresh, role security, RLS, MIS, capacity, and customer-confidentiality behavior is unchanged.

Release Delivery
- Publish matching root and db04 copies.
- Browser assets use the unique v65-db44 cache marker.
- The gear menu displays “DB44 · coloured identity and stable navigation active”.
