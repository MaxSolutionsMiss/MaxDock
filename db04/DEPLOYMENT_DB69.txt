MaxDock DB69 — Shared interface consistency

Scope
- Dashboard, Operation Queue, Operational Reports, and My Appointments now use one final UI contract.
- Field labels remain directly before their controls and cannot overlap the input or select.
- Dashboard action order is Date, Status, Book Appointment, Block Time, Date Range, then Export CSV, Print, Gear.
- Queue action order is Date, View, Today, Tomorrow, Refresh, then Full-Screen, Export CSV, Print, Gear.
- Reports keeps filters and Update on the left, with Export CSV, Print, Gear grouped at the far right.
- Export CSV and Print are the same 104 x 40 secondary buttons everywhere they appear.
- Gear controls are 40 x 40 and settings panels share the same menu treatment.
- KPI cards use a compact 78px horizontal layout and fill the available row.
- Turning off Show metrics/KPI dashboard collapses the entire KPI container, not just individual cards.
- My Appointments keeps the appointment list ahead of Notifications with a stable 10px section rhythm.

Safety
- Frontend-only release.
- No Supabase schema, policy, RPC, Edge Function, or data changes.
- Existing roles and three-minute live refresh behavior remain unchanged.
- Review only: do not merge or publish until approved.
