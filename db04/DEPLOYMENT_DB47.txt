MaxDock DB47 Preview — Compact left navigation and graphical operating cards

User-visible preview changes
- Moves the existing role-aware primary navigation from the top banner into a fixed, compact left-side rail on desktop.
- Keeps the top banner open for MaxDock branding, location selection, notifications, signed-in identity, and sign-out.
- Prevents page-specific location controls from moving the navigation because navigation no longer shares the top action lane.
- Adds restrained line icons and stronger functional colour to Dashboard, Operation Queue, My Appointments, and Reports KPI cards.
- Vertically centres Schedule Date, Appointment Status, range, queue, report, and appointment-view controls inside their cards.
- Reduces operational field widths and heights so controls are proportional instead of stretching across the toolbar.
- Centres the sign-in and password cards both vertically and horizontally.
- Restores the existing hamburger navigation below tablet width while hiding the desktop side rail.
