MaxDock v46-DB17 deployment notes

Database and Edge Functions
- No database migration is required after DB16 / schema v11.
- No Edge Function update is required.
- All new reports are calculated from the existing permission-filtered appointment data.

TV schedule
- Full-screen TV mode distributes visible dock lanes across the available screen height.
- Dock labels, appointment cards, and shipment details enlarge proportionately for broadcast displays.
- Longer appointment details wrap to a second line instead of being cut off.
- A large number of docks remains scrollable with a minimum readable row height.
- Exiting TV mode restores the standard Dashboard schedule proportions.

Operational Reports
- Report View includes Operations Overview, Truck Flow, Skid Movement, and Dock Utilization.
- Date Range includes Past 7 Days, Past 30 Days, This Month, and Custom Range.
- The featured chart, legend, daily summary columns, and CSV export change with the selected report view.
- Truck Flow separates inbound and outbound truck counts by date.
- Skid Movement separates inbound and outbound skid volume by date.
- Dock Utilization shows occupied capacity and a capacity-focused daily table.
- AI Operations Brief continues using the selected location and date range.

Settings
- Operating Hours and Timing Rules share a balanced top row.
- Dock Doors and Vehicle Compatibility spans the full Settings width.
- The dock table is more compact and its Action column remains visible while scrolling.

Deployment
- Upload the updated root files and the matching db04 files.
- Hard-refresh the browser after GitHub Pages finishes publishing.
