MaxDock DB49 — Consistent controls, complete KPI cards, and role-safe navigation

User-visible changes
- Prevents Dashboard, Operation Queue, My Appointments, and Reports KPI cards from being compressed or cropped by older density rules.
- Aligns field labels, inputs, selects, and adjacent buttons to one consistent bottom baseline across operational toolbars.
- Increases the desktop workspace gutter from 12px to 18px and adds balanced spacing between page-local navigation and its content.
- Removes explanatory subtext and account-count lines from page-local navigation menus.
- Removes all application navigation from Sign In and Set Password screens.
- Restricts Vendor accounts to My Appointments and makes My Appointments their landing page.
- Shows User Management and Data Integration only to System Admin accounts.
- Verifies User Management access through the database is_system_admin function; unauthorized users return to their permitted landing page instead of seeing a fatal overlay.
