MaxDock DB65 — coordinator navigation and final layout harmonization

User-visible changes
- Coordinator navigation is limited to Dashboard, Appointments, Queue, and Reports.
- Settings is removed for coordinator accounts, even if a legacy permission is present.
- Direct coordinator access to Settings redirects to Operation Queue.
- My Appointments places Book an Appointment directly beside the banner message and keeps the KPI gear at the far right.
- Five My Appointments KPI cards fill the full page width evenly.
- Show is directly before the Appointment List dropdown.
- Dashboard Schedule Date, Appointment Status, and Date Range labels sit directly before their fields.
- Queue Operating Date and Queue View labels sit directly before their fields.
- Reports Report View and Date Range labels sit directly before their fields.
- Reports KPI settings use normal-sized checkboxes, cleaner spacing, and a compact readable menu.
- Vertical spacing between banners, KPI rows, appointment sections, and report sections is standardized to 10px.
- The DB60 KPI enforcement runtime is retired and replaced by one scoped appointment-metrics observer.

Data and deployment impact
- Frontend-only release.
- No Supabase migration.
- No Edge Function deployment.
