MaxDock v46-DB23 Deployment

Changes
- Adds a staff-only custom appointment time option for System Admin, Site Admin, Shipping Manager, and Coordinator accounts with appointment-create permission.
- Warns when the proposed appointment falls outside the selected location's operating hours and requires an explicit confirmation before booking.
- Keeps Customer accounts restricted to normal capacity-ready appointment slots inside operating hours.
- Records whether the appointment used an after-hours override, who confirmed it, and when it was confirmed.
- Expands the Dashboard schedule timeline when an appointment starts early, ends late, or crosses midnight.
- Detects reverse intersite routes within 18 hours and shows potential return-load recommendations during booking, on Dashboard, and in Operations Queue.
- Keeps route consolidation advisory only; MaxDock never changes or merges appointments automatically.

Database
- Apply MaxDock_DB_v15_After_Hours_Return_Loads.sql using migration name maxdock_db23_after_hours_return_loads.
- The migration adds after-hours audit columns and installs the staff preview, updated booking, proposed-route matching, and scheduled-route opportunity RPCs.

Edge Functions
- No Edge Function deployment is required for DB23.

Frontend
- Publish all changed root files and their mirrored db04 copies.
- Hard-refresh the browser after GitHub Pages finishes deploying.

Verification
1. Sign in as a Coordinator or administrator, open Place Appointment, and confirm Staff Scheduling Override appears in Step 3.
2. Enable Use Custom Time, choose a time outside operating hours, and confirm the warning asks whether the choice is intentional.
3. Cancel the warning and confirm no time is selected; repeat and accept it, then confirm the Review step marks the audited override.
4. Sign in as a Customer and confirm no staff override controls are visible and outside-hours booking is rejected by the database.
5. Create or identify two structured MaxDock intersite appointments on reverse routes with non-overlapping times no more than 18 hours apart.
6. Confirm Potential Return Loads appears during booking and on Dashboard and Operations Queue.
7. Confirm each suggestion identifies both booking references and says it is a recommendation only.
8. Confirm after-hours appointments appear on the Dashboard timeline and carry an After Hours marker in the appointment list and Operations Queue.
