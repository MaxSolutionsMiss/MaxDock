MaxDock DB53 — Role-safe navigation, booking readiness, and compact appointment workspace

User-visible changes
- Limits Coordinator, Site Admin, and Shipping Manager navigation to Appointments, Queue, Reports, Dashboard, and Settings.
- Keeps Customer and Vendor accounts limited to My Appointments.
- Keeps User Management and Data Integration exclusive to System Admin accounts.
- Prevents booking from opening before role permissions finish loading, removing the false permission alert for Site Admin and Vendor accounts.
- Makes Operation Queue Refresh match the Book an Appointment and Save settings primary-button treatment.
- Removes the extra outer border and background around the Location control while retaining the dropdown border.
- Moves Appointment Views directly beneath Next Appointment, with filters on the left and the appointment list on the right.
- Removes descriptive subtext from Appointment Views and retains a compact phone/tablet layout.
