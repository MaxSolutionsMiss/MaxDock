MaxDock v56-DB35 — Visual Clarity and Professional Interface Refinement

Purpose
- Improve readability and visual polish without making MaxDock busier.
- Use a universal Arial/Helvetica font stack that works consistently across browsers and operating systems.
- Preserve DB33 operational density and DB34 spacing/alignment improvements.

Typography
- Arial is the primary interface font with Helvetica and generic sans-serif fallbacks.
- Heavy 750-style weights are replaced with standard 400, 500, 600, and 700 weights.
- Page headings, panel headings, form labels, table text, wizard labels, and supporting text use a consistent hierarchy.
- Filter labels use title case rather than small all-uppercase lettering.

Dashboard
- Today’s Queue, Place Appointment, and Block Time are equal 152 x 42 pixel actions on desktop.
- Primary action text is larger and centered horizontally and vertically.
- Schedule Date, Appointment Status, Date Range, Customize, and saved-view status sit inside a compact professional control shelf.
- Dashboard KPI labels increase to 12 pixels and use normal title case.
- Dashboard KPI values increase to 25 pixels.
- Export CSV and Print use matching 108-pixel widths.

Operations Queue
- Controls align at the top of the page header.
- Operating Date and Queue View remain first, followed by Today, Tomorrow, Refresh, Full screen, Customize, Export CSV, and Print.
- Frequent actions remain visually prominent while document actions remain secondary.
- Export CSV and Print use matching 108-pixel widths.
- Queue KPI labels and values are easier to scan.

Place Appointment
- Use and Delete buttons use matching 78-pixel widths.
- Step 1–5 labels use clearer Arial typography and a moderate semibold weight.
- Step headings, introductions, form labels, inputs, slot text, and navigation actions are easier to read.
- Staff scheduling override and Use custom time use lighter, clearer weights.
- DB34 white perimeter and interior breathing room remain unchanged.

Other Pages
- Reports, Settings, User Management, Data Integration, menus, tables, and supporting pages inherit the same font hierarchy and equal document-action sizing.
- Shadows are restrained and focus outlines remain visible.

Deployment
- Frontend only.
- No database migration.
- No Supabase Edge Function deployment.
- No appointment-processing or scheduling logic changes.
- Publish root and db04 copies of maxdock-config.js and maxdock-db35.css.
- DB35 uses the unique v56-db35 asset marker.
