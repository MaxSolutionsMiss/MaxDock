MaxDock v54-DB33 release summary

Dashboard
- Compact operational band combines date, status, range, customization, and saved-view status.
- Five primary KPI cards show by default; users may select one to six.
- More vertical room is available for dock schedules, including sites with six or seven doors.

Operations Queue
- Date, view, Today, Tomorrow, Refresh, Full screen, Customize, Export CSV, and Print share one compact header toolbar.
- Untouched eight-card metric defaults become five operational metrics; existing user customizations are preserved.
- Morning brief and execution sections use less vertical space.

Across MaxDock
- Shorter utility buttons, narrower filters, tighter page headers, reduced panel padding, and more compact tables.
- Responsive mobile layouts remain stacked and usable.

Deployment
- Frontend only.
- No database migration.
- No Edge Function deployment.
