MaxDock v47-DB26 Deployment

Changes
- Restores a staff-selectable Direction field with Outbound selected by default.
- Changes the route question and labels automatically when staff select Inbound.
- Keeps customer-role bookings inbound from an external customer or vendor origin.
- Reserves a real compatible dock at both Max Solutions sites for every internal transfer.
- Shows the same appointment window as Outbound at the sending site and Inbound at the receiving site.
- Places linked movements in the receiving site's actual dock lane instead of the generic Linked movement lane.
- Checks both dock schedules atomically so neither end can be double-booked.
- Uses the receiving site's hours, booking window, dock capacity, and skid-capacity rules when suggesting times.
- Preserves staff after-hours confirmation while validating dock availability at both internal sites.
- Backfills conflict-free counterpart docks for existing active internal transfers.
- Keeps WIP active and removes the accidental VIP appointment type from new bookings.

Database
- Apply MaxDock_DB_v18_Dual_Site_Dock_Routing.sql using migration name maxdock_db26_dual_site_dock_routing.
- The migration adds counterpart_dock_id, routed slot/preview/booking RPCs, cross-dock conflict enforcement, linked capacity projections, schedule dock projection, and VIP deactivation.
- No Edge Function deployment is required.

Frontend
- Publish all changed root files and their mirrored db04 copies.
- Hard-refresh the browser after GitHub Pages finishes deploying v47-db26 assets.

Verification
1. As a coordinator, open Place Appointment and confirm Direction defaults to Outbound but also offers Inbound.
2. Choose Max Solutions and confirm changing Direction switches the wording between destination and origin.
3. Book an internal transfer and confirm the shared window names a dock at both locations.
4. Open the sending location schedule and confirm the transfer is Outbound on its assigned dock.
5. Open the receiving location schedule and confirm the same transfer is Inbound on its own assigned dock.
6. Confirm the receiving dock is one of that location's configured docks and not a generic Linked movement lane.
7. Confirm a conflicting booking or dock block cannot reserve either assigned dock during the same window.
8. Confirm a Customer or Vendor external inbound booking reserves only the receiving Max Solutions dock.
9. Confirm WIP is available and VIP no longer appears in Appointment Type.
10. Confirm staff can intentionally approve an outside-hours time and customers remain limited to ordinary hours.
